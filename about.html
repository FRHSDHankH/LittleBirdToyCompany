Toy Quality

We believe in the power of simple, safe play. Our toys are handcrafted from sustainable Baltic birch wood—completely free from plastic and batteries. Every piece is carefully made and tested in our Toms River, NJ workshop to meet ASTM and CA Prop 65 safety standards and ensure lasting durability.

Sustainability

At Little Bird Toys, we’re dedicated to eco-friendly craftsmanship. Our timeless wooden toys are built to last, using a low-waste process that supports American manufacturing and protects the planet for future generations.
